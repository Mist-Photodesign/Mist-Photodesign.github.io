About me

As a visual artist, my primary focus lies in the domain of CGI, where I leverage my expertise in photography to provide a distinctive viewpoint to this medium. Drawing inspiration from this experience, I strive to create artworks that stand out and leave a lasting impression. My work spans across a variety of mediums, including video games, web development, and stand-alone CGI art pieces. My artistic vision is diverse, ranging from photorealistic product CGI to abstract geometric art. Sometimes, I combine these two styles to create stunning and unexpected outcomes. My upcoming exhibition at the Free Range Gallery in London (June - July of 2023) is a testament to the range of my work.

In my photorealistic CGI which depicts products designed by me, I focus on creating procedural materials that accurately depict real-life objects. I pay close attention to detail to create an authentic representation of the subject matter. While designing the products I conduct thorough research to ensure that my work is faithful to reality and also adds value by enhancing existing designs. In the pursuit of creating impactful product videos, I aim to infuse a distinctive touch by incorporating intricate fluid simulations, unorthodox lighting techniques, and captivating camera movements, often combining multiple techniques that synergistically come together to enhance the overall visual appeal of the final output.

My abstract CG art, on the other hand, explores the transformation of strong geometric shapes as well as bending reality to create a unique and mesmerizing experience. I aim to push the boundaries of what is possible within CGI and explore the abstract world with the art I produce.
